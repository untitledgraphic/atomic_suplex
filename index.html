Rock & Roll Action Now!

Born kicking and screaming through a fighter pilot's helmet microphone, Atomic Suplex quickly became known across Europe, America and the far east for their incendiary live performances, crashing around the stage (and beyond) in their own personal demolition derby. In full leather or practically naked, Atomic Suplex deliver undiluted punk rock and roll so fast and hard you'll need a helmet too.

With releases on Crypt, Dirty Water, Frantic City, Black Gladiator, One Little Indian, Radiowave, Swamp Fiction, Death Pop, and Little Water

Atomic Suplex have been honoured to share the stage with such luminaries as...

The Hives, The Datsuns, The Gories, Guitar Wolf, The Adolescents, Buzzcocks, The Fat White Family, The Schizophonics, Ed Tudor Pole, Shannon & The Clams, The Fadaways and many more.

Turn your amps up to 10 and fall backwards down the stairs... It's Atomic Suplex.
